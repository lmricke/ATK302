    A Little About me...

    My Name is Tyler Coda, and if you have found this site, then you may know a little about me already.
    I am an artist, with experience in many fields such as graphic design, sculpting, website development, video editing, painting, 3D modeling/rendering, and many others. I have worked in graphic design for many years now, both as a freelancer and in a professional setting. From simple business logos to event posters, I am capable of creating designs that are visually distinct yet coherent in delivering its message, that meet the expectations and needs of the client.
